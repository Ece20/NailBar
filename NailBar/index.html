What do we do ????

Prosthetic nail, It is a nail extension process performed by gluing the fake nail
on your own nail and shaping it. It lasts about three hours with a single color nail polish.
It can be used for a long time as long as it is taken care of.


Prosthetic nail, Manicure with permanent nail polish In our process, a dry manicure is done first,
then a base is applied to show the surface of the nail smooth and neat.
After the base, one color permanent nail polish or nail art is applied depending on preference.
Nail polishes are dried with the help of LED light.
It lasts about 2 hours with a single color nail polish.


Prosthetic nail, Aquarium Prosthetic Nail Aquarium Artificial Nail has the same procedure as gel
false nail treatment. Although it is very popular abroad,
it is not a very common type of nail art in Turkey. It can be used for a long time with regular maintenance.
The process takes four hours.
